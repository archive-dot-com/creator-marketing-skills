# 100 Net-New Creator Marketing Skill Ideas

Generated from 5 parallel research agents, each with a different lens. These are in addition to the existing 15 skills in `docs/skills-draft.md`.

All skills are Claude Code only — text-in, text-out, no browser required.

---

## Lens 1: Day-to-Day Workflow Automation (20 skills)

1. **Campaign Budget Allocator** — Takes total campaign budget, goals, and creator tier targets, then outputs a recommended budget breakdown across tiers, fees, gifting, and contingency with rationale.

2. **Creator Persona Profile Builder** — Transforms raw creator data (niche, audience demographics, past brand deals) into a structured persona card that helps teams quickly assess fit and brief alignment.

3. **Influencer Pitch Deck Outline Generator** — Builds a structured outline for internal or client-facing pitch decks presenting a proposed influencer campaign strategy, including talking points for each slide.

4. **Content Repurposing Planner** — Given a set of creator deliverables, generates a repurposing plan for owned channels (email, paid ads, organic social, website) including format adaptations and usage window recommendations.

5. **Posting Schedule Optimizer** — Takes campaign launch date, creator count, and platform mix, then outputs a staggered posting calendar with timing rationale to maximize reach and avoid audience fatigue.

6. **Creator Briefing FAQ Generator** — Anticipates and answers the most common creator questions about a campaign brief, reducing back-and-forth by producing a ready-to-send FAQ document.

7. **Red Flag Vetting Checklist** — Analyzes creator background notes, past controversies, and brand history to surface potential brand safety risks in a structured, shareable checklist format.

8. **Affiliate & Commission Structure Designer** — Builds a tiered affiliate commission structure based on campaign goals, creator tier, and product margins, with suggested payout mechanics and tracking instructions.

9. **Creator Re-Engagement Script Writer** — Writes personalized re-engagement messages for dormant creator relationships, referencing past collaborations and new campaign hooks to restart the conversation.

10. **Performance Benchmark Setter** — Takes campaign inputs (industry, platform, creator tier, budget) and outputs realistic KPI benchmarks for reach, engagement, conversions, and EMV to set expectations before launch.

11. **Contract Redline Response Generator** — Takes a creator or agency's requested contract changes and drafts professional, strategic counter-responses for each redline to accelerate negotiation cycles.

12. **Content Approval Feedback Formatter** — Converts internal team notes and raw feedback on creator content drafts into a clear, constructive, brand-voice-consistent revision request ready to send to the creator.

13. **Post-Campaign Creator Debrief Template** — Generates a structured debrief questionnaire to send creators after a campaign, capturing performance insights, audience feedback, and relationship health signals.

14. **Seasonal Campaign Calendar Builder** — Maps out a 12-month influencer activation calendar tied to brand tentpole moments, cultural events, and product launches with suggested creator tier and format by moment.

15. **Agency RFP Response Evaluator** — Takes multiple agency RFP responses and scores them against weighted criteria, outputting a comparison matrix and recommendation summary to accelerate vendor selection.

16. **Creator Tier Framework Designer** — Builds a custom creator tier system (nano through mega) with defined follower ranges, expected rates, deliverable expectations, and ideal use cases tailored to a specific brand category.

17. **Campaign Recap Email Writer** — Drafts polished recap emails to internal stakeholders or clients summarizing campaign performance, highlights, creator wins, and recommended next steps.

18. **Gifting Fulfillment Brief Generator** — Produces a logistics brief for the warehouse or fulfillment team detailing what to pack, personalization notes per creator, and shipping deadlines tied to campaign go-live dates.

19. **Creator Conflict-of-Interest Screener** — Takes a list of creator brand partnerships and flags direct category conflicts, competitor relationships, or exclusivity concerns relative to a target brand's requirements.

20. **Paid Amplification Brief Writer** — Converts an organic influencer deliverable into a whitelisting or dark post brief for the paid media team, including audience targeting rationale, copy variations, and creative direction notes.

## Lens 2: Pain Points & Problem Solving (20 skills)

21. **Creator Payment & Invoice Tracker** — Transforms a messy list of creator names, rates, and deliverable statuses into a structured payment schedule with outstanding balances, due dates, and approval flags, so nothing gets paid late or paid twice.

22. **Campaign ROI Calculator & Narrative Builder** — Takes raw inputs (spend, reach, engagement, promo codes, affiliate revenue) and outputs a clean ROI summary with the narrative framing leadership actually wants to hear.

23. **Deliverable Deadline Chaser Script** — Generates a sequence of personalized follow-up messages (gentle reminder, firm reminder, final notice) for creators who are late on posts, stories, or content submissions.

24. **Content Approval Feedback Formatter** — Converts rough internal notes like "this feels off-brand" into clear, actionable, professional feedback messages ready to send directly to creators or their managers.

25. **Seeding List Builder & Prioritizer** — Takes a product launch brief and a list of creators and outputs a tiered gifting priority list with personalization notes, quantities, and suggested messaging for each tier.

26. **Creator Contract Scope Checker** — Pastes in a contract or deal memo and flags missing clauses, ambiguous deliverable language, and common loopholes around usage rights, exclusivity windows, and kill fees.

27. **Campaign Status Dashboard Digest** — Takes a raw data dump from a spreadsheet (creator, platform, post status, metrics) and generates a clean weekly status summary formatted for Slack, email, or a leadership meeting.

28. **Whitelisting & Paid Amplification Brief** — Generates the internal and creator-facing documentation needed to set up whitelisting or dark posting from a creator's handle, including timeline, budget, and approval steps.

29. **Creator Reactivation Outreach Generator** — Identifies lapsed creator relationships from a provided history list and writes personalized re-engagement messages referencing past work and new campaign hooks.

30. **Budget Reallocation Advisor** — Takes mid-campaign performance data and outputs a reasoned recommendation for shifting spend between underperforming and overperforming creators or channels.

31. **UGC Content Repurposing Roadmap** — Given a list of approved creator content assets, generates a structured plan for where and how to reuse each piece across paid ads, email, PDPs, and organic social.

32. **Creator Onboarding Checklist Generator** — Produces a complete, campaign-specific onboarding packet for new creators covering brand guidelines, posting windows, hashtags, FTC rules, and contact info — ready to paste into an email.

33. **Post-Campaign Creator Scorecard** — Scores each creator on a campaign across consistency, content quality, engagement rate, and brand alignment, producing a ranked retention list for future campaigns.

34. **Internal Campaign Briefing Deck Outline** — Converts a campaign brief into a structured slide-by-slide outline for an internal kickoff or leadership presentation, including talking points for each section.

35. **Agency vs. In-House Cost Comparison Builder** — Takes current agency fees and internal headcount costs and produces a side-by-side analysis to help brands decide whether to bring creator marketing in-house.

36. **Creator Tier Structure Designer** — Builds a tiered creator strategy (nano, micro, macro, mega) for a given budget and campaign goal, with recommended splits, expected reach, and trade-off rationale.

37. **Story & Ephemeral Content Capture Brief** — Generates instructions and a tracking template for creators to self-report story metrics, screen recordings, and swipe-up data before content disappears, reducing manual chasing.

38. **Competitive Spend & Share-of-Voice Estimator** — Takes publicly observable campaign signals (visible posts, creator tiers, estimated rates) from competitor brands and outputs a rough share-of-voice and spend estimate.

39. **Legal & PR Risk Flagging Checklist** — Reviews a campaign concept or creator's recent public content against a checklist of brand safety, legal, and reputational risk criteria, surfacing issues before contracts are signed.

40. **Creator Marketing Hiring Brief Generator** — Produces a job description, interview question set, and skills rubric for hiring an in-house influencer manager, tailored to the brand's category, team size, and campaign volume.

## Lens 3: Platform & Channel Expertise (20 skills)

41. **Instagram Reels Brief Optimizer** — Takes a campaign concept and outputs a Reels-specific content brief with hook timing recommendations, trending audio cue suggestions, caption length guidance, and CTA placement strategy.

42. **TikTok Hook Writer** — Generates 10+ scroll-stopping opening lines (first 1–3 seconds of script) tailored to a specific product category and target audience, using proven TikTok hook formulas (curiosity gap, bold claim, relatability opener).

43. **Cross-Platform Content Adaptation Guide** — Takes a single campaign concept and outputs a platform-by-platform execution plan (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Pinterest) with format, length, tone, caption, and CTA adjusted for each.

44. **YouTube Shorts vs. Long-Form Strategy Planner** — Given a product or campaign goal, recommends how to split creator deliverables between Shorts and long-form, with rationale based on funnel stage, product complexity, and audience intent.

45. **TikTok Shop Listing Copy Generator** — Produces product titles, bullet descriptions, and affiliate creator talking points optimized for TikTok Shop discovery, combining SEO-style keywords with conversational language that converts in-feed.

46. **Hashtag Strategy Builder** — Generates a tiered hashtag set (niche, mid-size, broad) per platform and post type, with guidance on quantity, placement (caption vs. comment), and rotation strategy to avoid shadowban patterns.

47. **Instagram Carousel Script & Flow Planner** — Structures a multi-slide carousel for a creator or brand, including slide-by-slide copy, visual direction, swipe-prompt language, and final-slide CTA optimized for saves and shares.

48. **Platform Algorithm Briefing Sheet** — Produces a plain-language summary of current known ranking signals and content best practices for a chosen platform, formatted as a one-page creator briefing document.

49. **Whitelisting & Dark Post Setup Guide** — Generates a step-by-step whitelisting request workflow for a specific platform (Meta or TikTok), including ask language for creator outreach, creative specs, and audience targeting recommendations.

50. **TikTok vs. Instagram Reels Benchmarking Framework** — Takes raw performance inputs and outputs a normalized benchmark comparison across both platforms, flagging which metrics matter most per platform context.

51. **Pinterest Creator Content Strategy Template** — Outputs a Pinterest-specific content plan for a creator partnership, including board strategy, pin format recommendations, keyword-rich description templates, and seasonal timing guidance.

52. **YouTube Community Post & Shorts Caption Writer** — Drafts community tab posts and Shorts captions designed to drive clicks, comments, and subscriptions, with platform-native language and CTA structures.

53. **Stories Sequence Planner** — Maps out a creator's Instagram or Snapchat Stories arc for a sponsored moment — slide count, content type per slide (poll, question sticker, swipe-up, countdown), pacing, and narrative flow.

54. **Spark Ads / Boosted Post Creative Brief** — Generates a creative brief for converting an organic creator post into a paid TikTok Spark Ad or Meta-boosted post, including audience targeting rationale and KPI expectations.

55. **Platform-Specific Disclosure Language Generator** — Outputs the correct disclosure language, placement instructions, and format requirements for sponsored content, tailored to platform UI constraints and current FTC guidance.

56. **Creator Content Repurposing Playbook** — Takes one hero piece of creator content and maps out how to repurpose it across six or more touchpoints (email, paid ad, website, Pinterest, YouTube Shorts, brand feed repost).

57. **Affiliate Link & Promo Code Performance Analyzer** — Takes affiliate or promo code data and outputs a structured analysis identifying top-performing creators, content formats, and platforms, with recommended budget reallocation.

58. **TikTok Trending Sound & Format Briefing** — Generates a weekly creative briefing template for creators, summarizing trending audio, video formats, and content styles gaining traction in a specific niche category.

59. **Platform Content Calendar Template Generator** — Builds a platform-specific posting calendar for a creator campaign — posting frequency, content type rotation, optimal windows, and spacing rules to avoid content fatigue.

60. **Engagement Rate Benchmarking & Red Flag Detector** — Takes a creator's metrics and outputs a benchmark comparison, quality score, and flagged anomalies (comment-to-like ratio, follower spike patterns, engagement drop indicators).

## Lens 4: Strategy, Analysis & Business Impact (20 skills)

61. **Creator Program Maturity Scorecard** — Assesses your current influencer program across 10 dimensions (strategy, tech stack, measurement, team, etc.) and outputs a maturity level with a prioritized roadmap to reach the next stage.

62. **Annual Creator Budget Planner** — Takes your total marketing budget, business goals, and channel mix inputs and outputs a recommended creator marketing budget allocation with rationale for leadership sign-off.

63. **ROI Forecast Model** — Given campaign inputs (spend, creator tier, product price, audience size), generates a projected ROI range with assumptions, sensitivity analysis, and a pre-campaign benchmark.

64. **Creator Program Business Case Builder** — Structures a full executive-ready business case for launching or expanding a creator program, including market sizing, competitive benchmarks, projected returns, and investment ask.

65. **Channel Mix Optimizer** — Analyzes your current creator spend across platforms and recommends reallocation based on category benchmarks, your goals, and audience behavior patterns.

66. **Quarterly Creator Program Review** — Transforms raw campaign performance data into a structured QBR document with wins, misses, trend analysis, and recommended pivots for the next quarter.

67. **Creator Tier Strategy Builder** — Defines the right mix of nano, micro, macro, and mega creators for your specific category, budget, and growth stage, with expected tradeoffs for each tier combination.

68. **Audience Overlap Analyzer** — Takes creator audience demographic and interest inputs and flags redundancy across your current roster, recommending diversification to maximize unique reach per dollar spent.

69. **Attribution Model Selector** — Walks through your business model, sales cycle, and tracking capabilities to recommend the right attribution approach and how to implement it without a data team.

70. **Agency vs. In-House Decision Framework** — Evaluates your program scale, team capacity, and budget against industry benchmarks to produce a build-vs-buy recommendation with cost comparisons and transition plan.

71. **Creator Program KPI Framework** — Generates a custom measurement framework aligned to your business stage and goals, mapping leading and lagging indicators to executive-level metrics that translate to revenue impact.

72. **Competitive Creator Spend Estimator** — Takes competitor brand inputs and publicly available signals to estimate their creator investment, platform focus, and roster strategy, surfacing whitespace opportunities.

73. **Seasonal Campaign Calendar Builder** — Maps your product launch calendar, cultural moments, and category purchase cycles into a prioritized annual creator activation plan with recommended budget phasing.

74. **Creator Category Expansion Planner** — Given your current creator category focus, identifies adjacent verticals worth testing, ranks them by audience overlap and conversion potential, and proposes a pilot framework.

75. **Team Structure & Hiring Roadmap** — Based on your program size and growth targets, recommends the right in-house creator team structure, role sequencing, and headcount timing with estimated cost-to-build.

76. **Creator Program Post-Mortem Generator** — Converts end-of-year campaign data and qualitative inputs into a structured retrospective that surfaces systemic issues, celebrates wins, and feeds into next-year planning.

77. **Influencer Marketing Benchmark Report Builder** — Compiles your internal performance data against category benchmarks (CPE, EMV, conversion rates) to produce a gap analysis that justifies budget increases or strategic shifts.

78. **Creator Contract Risk Auditor** — Reviews contract terms across your active creator agreements and flags concentration risk, exclusivity gaps, usage rights conflicts, and renewal vulnerabilities.

79. **Leadership Presentation Deck Outline** — Takes campaign results or program strategy inputs and generates a narrative-driven slide-by-slide outline designed to secure executive buy-in, not just report metrics.

80. **Market Sizing & Opportunity Analyzer** — Given your product category, target consumer, and geographic focus, estimates the addressable creator marketing opportunity and sizes the gap between current investment and market potential.

## Lens 5: Creative, Content & Relationship Management (20 skills)

81. **Creator Content Concept Generator** — Takes a product, campaign theme, and creator's niche/aesthetic to generate 5–10 tailored content concepts that feel native to the creator's style rather than brand-dictated.

82. **Talking Points & Script Outliner** — Converts a campaign brief and product details into a flexible script outline or talking point sheet for creators, balancing brand messaging with room for authentic voice.

83. **Content Revision Request Writer** — Transforms blunt internal feedback ("this feels too salesy") into diplomatic, specific, and actionable revision requests that preserve the creator relationship and get to approval faster.

84. **Creator Onboarding Welcome Kit** — Generates a personalized onboarding document for a new creator partner: brand story, tone-of-voice guide, do's and don'ts, key contacts, and timeline — written warmly to set the relationship off right.

85. **UGC Repurposing Brief** — Takes existing creator content descriptions and outputs a structured brief for the paid media or creative team: which clips to pull, how to adapt for ads, email, PDPs, and what copy angles to pair.

86. **Creator Anniversary & Milestone Message Writer** — Drafts personalized relationship-nurturing messages for moments like a creator's 1-year partnership anniversary, follower milestone, or viral moment — the touchpoints that build loyalty.

87. **Influencer Event Run-of-Show Builder** — Takes an event concept, guest list of creators, and brand objectives and outputs a detailed run-of-show: arrival flow, content moment cues, talking points, gifting sequence, and creator-specific personalization.

88. **Brand Safety & Content Guidelines Document** — Generates a clear, brand-specific content guidelines doc covering restricted claims, ingredient sensitivities, competitor mentions, visual standards, and tone boundaries.

89. **Campaign Hashtag & Caption Strategy** — Develops a tiered hashtag strategy and sample caption frameworks for a campaign, segmented by creator tier and platform, so creators have direction without sounding copy-pasted.

90. **Cross-Creator Campaign Narrative Mapper** — For campaigns with multiple creators, builds a storytelling arc across the roster — who leads with awareness, who drives consideration, who closes with conversion.

91. **Creator Mood Board Brief** — Translates a campaign concept into a written aesthetic direction document: color palette descriptors, lighting references, wardrobe notes, location vibes, and editing style guidance.

92. **Content Calendar Template Generator** — Builds a customized posting schedule for a creator partnership: publish dates, platform, content type, review deadlines, and go-live windows — formatted for easy handoff.

93. **Post-Campaign Creator Feedback Letter** — Drafts a thoughtful, constructive feedback summary to share with creators after a campaign — celebrating what worked, noting what to adjust, and investing in the relationship.

94. **Creator Tier Communication Style Guide** — Generates tone and communication guidelines for managing creators at different tiers, helping junior team members know when to be more hands-on vs. hands-off.

95. **Community Engagement Prompt Pack** — Produces 15–20 comment reply starters, community challenge prompts, and engagement bait captions for creators or brand community managers to drive authentic interaction.

96. **Gifting Personalization Note Writer** — Takes a creator's known interests, past content, and product being gifted to write a handwritten-style personalization note — turning a PR mailer into a memorable moment.

97. **Creator Re-Engagement Script** — For creators who've gone quiet or whose performance has dipped, generates a check-in message sequence designed to re-energize the relationship and surface what's not working.

98. **UGC Usage Rights Tracker & Renewal Reminder Doc** — Builds a structured reference document listing which creators' content has been licensed, for which channels, at what expiration dates, and drafts renewal outreach messages.

99. **Paid Partnership Disclosure Caption Rewriter** — Takes a creator's draft caption and rewrites it to be FTC-compliant while preserving their voice and engagement potential — a practical companion to compliance review.

100. **Evergreen Creator Brief Template Library** — Generates a set of reusable brief templates for the most common creator content formats (GRWM, tutorial, unboxing, haul, day-in-the-life, review) tailored to a brand's product category and tone.
