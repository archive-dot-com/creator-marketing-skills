# Skills v1 Manifest — 30 Skills

The first batch of creator marketing skills for Archive. Organized into 8 categories covering the full creator marketing workflow.

---

## A. Discovery & Vetting (3)

**1. Audience Demographic Analyzer**
Paste in an influencer's stats or platform export and get a structured breakdown of whether their audience aligns with your target demo, with a pass/fail verdict.

**2. Brand Safety Screen**
Input an influencer's recent content and receive a brand safety analysis flagging political controversy, offensive language, sensitive topics, or past scandal indicators.

**3. Niche Fit Scorer**
Describe your brand and campaign, then paste in an influencer's bio and content — the skill scores niche fit on a 1-10 scale with written rationale.

---

## B. Outreach & Negotiation (5)

**4. Creator Outreach Sequence Generator**
Asks about your brand, campaign goals, target creator, and preferred format (email, Instagram DM, TikTok DM, etc.), then generates a multi-touch outreach sequence with personalization hooks and follow-up cadence.

**5. Reply Triage Classifier**
Paste in a batch of influencer replies and get each classified (interested, negotiating, declined, ghosted, needs info) with a suggested next action for each.

**6. Creator Rate Estimator**
Asks for the creator's niche, follower count, engagement rate, platform, and requested deliverables, then provides rate ranges with reasoning based on tier, category, and market benchmarks.

**7. Creator Negotiation Assistant**
Walks through negotiation scenarios — paste in a counter-offer, objection, or stalled deal and get strategic response options with trade-off analysis and suggested language.

**8. Verbal Agreement Summarizer**
Paste in a DM or email thread where terms were discussed and receive a clean bulleted summary of agreed terms formatted to send back for confirmation.

---

## C. Campaign Setup & Briefing (5)

**9. Universal Creator Follow-Up Chaser**
One skill for all chasing scenarios. Pick the situation — missing info, unsigned contract, late content, missing metrics, or incomplete whitelisting setup — provide creator details, and get a personalized follow-up sequence with escalating tone.

**10. UTM Parameter Builder**
Takes campaign name, source, medium, and creator handle and generates correctly formatted UTM strings and full tracking URLs for each creator.

**11. Creator Briefing FAQ Generator**
Anticipates and answers the most common creator questions about a campaign brief, reducing back-and-forth by producing a ready-to-send FAQ document.

**12. Creator Content Concept Generator**
Takes a product, campaign theme, and creator's niche/aesthetic to generate 5-10 tailored content concepts that feel native to the creator's style rather than brand-dictated.

**13. Multi-Platform Format Adapter**
Takes a single master brief and outputs platform-specific versions (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) with adjusted specs, aspect ratios, and platform-specific do's and don'ts.

---

## D. Content Review & Compliance (3)

**14. Content Approval Feedback Formatter**
Converts internal team notes and raw feedback on creator content drafts into a clear, constructive, brand-voice-consistent revision request ready to send to the creator.

**15. Content-to-Brief Compliance Checker**
Takes a brief and submitted content description and produces a structured pass/fail checklist against every brief requirement.

**16. FTC & Disclosure Spot-Checker**
Reviews a submitted caption or script for disclosure compliance and flags issues with specific fixes.

---

## E. Metrics & Monitoring (4)

**17. Story Metrics Screenshot Parser**
Paste raw text from a Story insights screenshot and this skill formats it into a clean, spreadsheet-ready row with labeled fields.

**18. Metrics Normalization Formatter**
Paste messy metrics from multiple sources and receive a single normalized table with standardized field names ready to merge into your master tracker.

**19. Content Capture Checklist Builder**
Takes activated influencers and deliverables and generates a monitoring checklist specifying what to look for on each platform and when to check.

**20. Engagement Rate Calculator & Benchmarker**
Input metrics for posts and receive calculated engagement rates plus benchmark comparisons against typical rates for that tier and platform.

---

## F. Reporting & ROI (4)

**21. Campaign ROI Calculator & Narrative Builder**
Takes raw inputs (spend, reach, engagement, promo codes, affiliate revenue) and outputs a clean ROI summary with the narrative framing leadership actually wants to hear.

**22. Campaign Status Dashboard Digest**
Takes raw spreadsheet data and generates a clean weekly status summary formatted for Slack, email, or a leadership meeting.

**23. Creator Posting Compliance Tracker**
Input contracted creators with required vs. actual post counts and receive a compliance table with escalation notes for non-compliant creators.

**24. Post-Campaign Creator Scorecard**
Scores each creator on a campaign across consistency, content quality, engagement rate, and brand alignment, producing a ranked retention list for future campaigns.

---

## G. Repurposing & Paid Media (3)

**25. Organic Repost Caption Writer**
Input the original caption, creator handle, and brand voice and receive 3 ready-to-post captions for reposting to your brand's channels with proper credit.

**26. Paid Social Creative Brief from Creator Content**
Takes a whitelisted post and generates a brief for the paid social team covering hook, messaging angle, audience fit, caption variants, and placement recommendations.

**27. Paid Ad Copy Adapter**
Input a creator's original caption or script and receive 3-5 adapted ad copy variations optimized for awareness, consideration, and conversion objectives.

---

## H. Strategy & Planning (3)

**28. Campaign Goal-to-KPI Framework Builder**
Input your business objective and get a full KPI framework with primary and secondary metrics, measurement approach, and benchmarks by creator tier.

**29. Performance Benchmark Setter**
Takes campaign inputs (industry, platform, creator tier, budget) and outputs realistic KPI benchmarks for reach, engagement, conversions, and EMV to set expectations before launch.

**30. Quarterly Creator Program Review**
Transforms raw campaign performance data into a structured QBR document with wins, misses, trend analysis, and recommended pivots for the next quarter.
